What I Do

High Visibility, High Impact

From the core shopping flow at ModCloth to the core source of revenue at Gild, I have managed many high visibility products. Ask me how I've worked with C-level stakeholders, or how I drove cross functional product launches.

Ship Quickly, Learn Quickly

I purposely structure my backlogs to deliver value as quickly as possible in order to measure success and iterate as needed. Ask me how I've used A/B tests, analytics and user research to ensure my product is making customers win.

Organize the Chaos

Turning lack of process, technical debt, and uncharted territory into streamlined product delivery is my specialty. Ask me how I cut release time in half at ModCloth, or how I established communication about Gild's features to stakeholders and customers.

A Few Products I've Managed

Core Shopping Experience [ModCloth]

My team owned the core shopping experience across phone, tablet and desktop. Delivered high profile features including redesigned navigation, homepage, filters and product detail pages which required extensive user research and communication to company-wide stakeholders. Balanced bug fixes and refactoring due to technical debt while still delivering on key metrics.

Seasonal Marketing Campaigns [ModCloth]

Managed development team that created seasonal campaigns for the marketing team. Delivered stylebooks for merchandising campaigns, marketing campaigns (including Holiday push) and key partnerships with celebrities like Kate Nash. Implemented new responsive blog with complete visual redesign and new platform (for ModCloth) - Wordpress.

Source [Gild]

Source uses data science, social media, and predictive analytics to help recruiters find the right talent, and nurture the relationships needed to make a hire. I managed A/B tests for incremental improvements while balancing major technical updates to integrate with the new Platform product offering.

Mission Control [Gild]

Along with the technical, I took on the operational shift from supporting one app to supporting a suite of apps by building a new internal tool called Mission Control. It is used daily by customer success team of 10 and improved demos for the sales team and training/onboarding for the Customer Success team.
